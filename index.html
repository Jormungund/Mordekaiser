Mordekaiser

Mordekaisers Q empowers his next three basic attacks with the 3rd's bonus doubled.


Mordekaisers W provides an area around him and an ally that will deal damage to all enemies in the range if the are touching. It can be re-activated or at the end of it's duration it will deal damage and steal back some health.


Mordekaisers E is an area effect damage skill that massively increases his passives shield.


Mordekaisers R places a curse on an enemy champion that does percent health damage over time. If the enemy champion dies during the duration it will become a ghost that Mordekaiser can control with either pressing r while hovering over your target or control + clicking.
